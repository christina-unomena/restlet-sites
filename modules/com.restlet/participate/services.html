<#global content> <@h3 "Offering" "offering">

To help you realize your Web ideas and succeed with your web APIs projects, Restlet offers a complete set of professional services including:

  • Subscription plans, for both Restlet framework and APISpark,
  • Consulting, such as co-development, API workshops and training.

Those services are directly related to our suite of products or our core expertise in RESTful web APIs.

Finally, you can consult the list of our latest customer references for those services.

<@h3 "Restlet Framework" "restlet-framework">

For Restlet Framework, our open source technology, we offer yearly plans that enable you to develop your web APIs more efficiently, and to deploy them in production with solid technical support in case of emergency.

In addition to the free software that can be downloaded and used with our open source licenses, and our community forums, your subscription includes:

  • technical support : several levels of service available
  • legal protection : we take responsibility in case of IP related issues
  • qualified versions : know which one to use in production

Please contact us for additional details.

<@h3 "APISpark" "apispark">

For APISpark, our all-in-one cloud platform (PaaS) for web APIs, we offer monthly subscription plans that let you create, host, manage and use web APIs.

In addition, we offer custom subscription plans for enterprises that need:

  • a single tenant (dedicated) platform instance,
  • a dedicated APIs catalog (based on the public one).
  • custom branding (white label UI),

Please contact us for additional details.

<@h3 "Mission types" "mission-types">

Here is a list of the main types of mission that we can offer. This list isn't exhaustive and a mission can combine several aspects:

  • Animation of API design workshops
  • Training on APIs, Restlet Framework and APISpark

Please contact us for additional details.
